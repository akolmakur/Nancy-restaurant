About Us

Nancy’s Restaurant is a family-owned South Sudanese and Sudanese restaurant located in the heart of Kilimani, Nairobi. We proudly serve traditional dishes that carry the rich heritage and flavors of our homeland.

Our story began with a passion for sharing the unique taste of home with the people of Kenya and the world. Each dish is prepared with authentic ingredients, traditional recipes, and a lot of love — just the way our grandmothers used to cook.

Whether you're a fan of asida, kisra, or ful medames, or trying Sudanese food for the first time, Nancy’s Restaurant promises you a warm welcome and a memorable dining experience.

Come visit us and taste the culture, the passion, and the stories behind every bite.

2025 Nancy's Restaurant. Kilimani, Nairobi, Kenya.
